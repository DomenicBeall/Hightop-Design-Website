About Us
Want to know about us and why we do what we do? You're in the right place.
Our Mission
Our mission is to help businesses small and large convey their passion for what they do with beautifully designed custom websites and digital media, because we love what we do and we’ll ensure you love it too.
Our Story
Hightop design and development was created by two University students and life-long friends, one with a keen interest in design, the other with a penchant for web development. We started by freelancing and soon developed a passion for quality web design. Having come from families that own small businesses we understand the difficulty of establishing an online presence and have since dedicated ourselves to helping small businesses make a big impression.
