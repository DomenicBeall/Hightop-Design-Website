What is a custom website?
A custom website is exactly that, custom. Unlike a template a custom website is created with your business and users in mind the whole time, every part of the site will be designed specifically for your business from the looks, feel, interactivity to bring your passion to life for your customers.
Difference between custom and template...
The difference is simple, with a template you are restricted only to basic tools and functions but at the end of the day you will have something everyone has. With a Custom Website it is the total opposite, the sky’s the limit.
Benefits of a custom website
Your passion, online!
We know your passionate about your business, so a custom website is the perfect way to represent that passion online. Typically, a website is the first point of call when customers are looking to get in contact so having a website that exactly represents you down to every last button, font and image is a huge advantage.
Designed for you
Our websites are designed with your goals in mind, whether you’re trying to sell your product, bring people into your restaurant or simply have an online presence that conveys professionalism, we design with intentionality to ensure that your site achieves your goals.
A never-ending story!
A Custom website with us means its we will work with you to grow and develop your site as your business does too. If your goals or business changes in a way you want reflected its as simple as letting us know.
How we do it
We follow a simple process that starts with understanding what you’re trying to achieve with your digital presence. We then block out how we’re going to achieve your goal before creating a series of initial designs. When we’re satisfied we consult with you to ensure satisfaction. After which we build your site with industry leading tools, ensuring it is reactive and accessible on all platforms. When you’re satisfied with the final product it’s put out into the world to achieve your goals and look good doing it.
